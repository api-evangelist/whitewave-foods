# WhiteWave Foods

The WhiteWave Foods Company was a consumer packaged food and beverage company headquartered in Denver, Colorado. It offered plant-based foods and beverages, coffee creamers, dairy, and organic produce under brands including Silk, So Delicious, Horizon Organic, International Delight, LAND O LAKES, Vega, Wallaby Organic, and Earthbound Farm.

WhiteWave was founded in 1977 and acquired by Danone in April 2017, becoming part of Danone North America (formerly DanoneWave). The company does not offer a public developer API.
